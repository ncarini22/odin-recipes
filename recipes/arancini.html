Arancini

Description

An arancini is one of Italy's most famous and recognizable snacks. One primarily consists of rice, ricotta cheese, breadcrumbs, and sometimes the addition of peas/ground beef. It is most definitely an easy snack to make and may be one to add to your own list of personal recipes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tabelspoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small onion, finely choped
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 cup uncooked Arborio rice
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 2 ½ cups boiling chicken stock
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 ounces finely chopped ham
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 4 ounces mozzarella cheese, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying, or as needed
    •

    Steps:

    1. Gather all ingredients
    2. Preheat oven to 200 degrees F
    3. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in pan on medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook until soft.
    4. Pour in rice and cook until lightly toasted.
    5. Stir in wine, stirring occasionally, until evaporated.
    6. Add hot chicken stock to rice, stirring and cooking until liquid has evaporated.
    7. Stir in peas and ham, then season with salt and pepper.
    8. Remove from heat and stir in Parmesan cheese.
    9. Beat 1 egg in a small bowl; stir into risotto.
    10. Beat remaining egg with milk in the same bowl. Place flour in bowl; place bread crumbs in a third bowl.
    11. Roll 2 tbsp of rice into ball. Press 1 mozzarella cube into the center and roll to enclose.
    12. Coat lightly with flour, dip into milk mixture, then roll breadcrumbs to coat.
    13. Heat 1 cup vegetable oil in a deep-fryer to 350 degrees F.
    14. Work in batches, fry until evenly golden brown.
    15. Drain on paper-lined plate and keep warm in oven.
    16. Enjoy!
